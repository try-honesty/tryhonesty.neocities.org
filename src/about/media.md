## Artwork
I'm a furry (shock horror); so obviously I like anthropomorphic animal themes in stuff.<br/>
But in a more general sense; well I have a pretty broad idea of what I consider art to begin with...<br/>
Traditional 'drawing' and 'painting' artworks, but also typography, photography, videography,<br/>
UI/UX, code-as-art...<br/>
As far as aesthetics; I tend to slant a bit modernist and minimalist,<br/>
while picking up on things of nostalgic, and thus sentimental value to me, like skeumorphism.<br/>
I quite like grunge and punk looks, but I don't know if their proprietors like me.<br/>
(Although if you ask me; to gatekeep it, as it were, would certainly go against their own ethoses, surely...)<br/>
Hasn't stopped me adding those elements to my own characters or such, of course.<br/><br/>
